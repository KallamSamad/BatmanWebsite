Batman

Summary

I am vengeance, I am the night, I am Batman!

After losing both of his parents to a tragic and, albeit, petty crime, Bruce devotes his entire life to make sure it is completely eliminated. He is a billionaire and the head of Wayne Enterprises - the company his father left him after he died. But he leads a double life; by day he is a CEO and by night he is Batman. He uses his wealth and skills to keep Gotham's streets safe, by taking out supervillains and thugs to keep the streets clean. Batman is armed with an arsenal of gadgets and abilities; he is the only hope for Gotham.
